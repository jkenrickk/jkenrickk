### 🎓 About Me

I am a final-year **BSc Mathematics and Data Science** student at the **University of British Columbia**, graduating **with distinction** (Class of 2026). I am the recipient of a **fully funded four-year scholarship from the Indonesian Ministry of Education**, awarded in recognition of my undergraduate research on stock price prediction which also won a **🥇 Gold Medal** at the National Student Research Competition (LKTIN).

My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, financial analytics, hydrology, and applied AI — building systems that turn messy real-world data into reliable, production-ready insights.
